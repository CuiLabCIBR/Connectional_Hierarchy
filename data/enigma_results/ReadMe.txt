For 22q11.2 deletion syndrome, attention-deficit/hyperactivity disorder, autism spectrum disorder, epilepsy (idiopathic generalized, right temporal lobe, and left temporal lobe), depression, obsessive-compulsive disorder, schizophrenia, and bipolar disorder, their cortical thickness and surface area data were obtained from the Enhancing Neuroimaging Genetics through Meta-Analysis (ENIGMA) consortium (Thompson et al., 2020) using the ENIGMA toolbox (https://github.com/MICA-MNI/ENIGMA) (Larivière et al., 2021).
The cortical atrophy data of obesity was obtained from the Supplementary Table 14 from (Opel et al., 2021).
The cortical atrophy data of schizotypy was obtained from the Supplementary Table 17 and 18 from (Kirschner et al., 2022).
The cortical atrophy data of Parkinson’s disease was obtained from the Supplementary Table 2a and 2b from (Laansma et al., 2021).
